          Benison Business Company Limited was founded in 1989 and has since been providing world - class adhesives and sealants of the finest quality to the market. First starting within the woodworking industry with our Cyano-Acrylate and Hotmelt products, we have expanded our product range to include silicones, acrylics and hybrid sealants, establishing our BRAVOTM brand as a market leader in the industry.
          In 1993, Benison Corporation was formed, leading to the establishment of Benison Group as the parent company of both Benison Business and Benison Corporation. With our main focus on providing better solutions for the evergrowing air-conditioning industry, we brought our MOMOTM Air-Conditioning Trunking System to the market. Ever since then, we have grown our MOMOTM brand into a market leader in the air-conditioning industry.
