BRAVO 929 : INTERIOR/EXTERIOR GENERAL SILICONE SEALANT

One part ready to use, multi-color available, excellent weather resistant, do not crack, shrink or chalk. BRAVO 929 silicone sealant is a cost effective general purpose, acetic cure formulation offering long term durability in a range of general sealing and general glazing application. It is extremely well suited for non-porous materials like, glass, aluminum, fiberglass, non-oily wood, metal and some plastic, Etc.

DIRECTIONS

Prepare clean, dry surface, cut tip and attach nozzle, cut nozzle at an angle to desire bead size, apply with caulking gun forcing sealant in the joint. Tool immediately, sealant skin in 5-10 minutes, is touch dry in 1 hour and cure to a depth of 7 mm in 7 days. Full bond strength is achieved in 7 days. Uncured material may be removed using a cloth and solvent (eg. Mineral turpentine). Fully cured excess materials may be removed by trimming with a sharp blade. Avoid under cutting the seal while curing a vinegar odour is released, Once cured this odour is disappears.

CAUTIONS

Releases acetic during cure, which may irritate the eyes, skin and respiratory tract. Keep away from eyes. In case of eye contact, flush with water immediately for 20 minutes and consult a physician. Avoid skin contract. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.

Storage:   Store below 90OF (30OC) in a dry place. Before handling, read product. Technical Data Sheet and Material Safety Data Sheet for detailed use and health information. Use Limitations: Not recommended for use the concrete, marble, oily wood – not paintable, not for structural glazing.
